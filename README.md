# North Woodmere Lawn Co-op 2026

A community-organized lawn care co-op for North Woodmere, Nassau County, NY.

The idea: 30+ homes sign up together, negotiate with local vendors as a block, and everyone saves $200–$400+ per year.

## How It Works

1. Neighbors fill out the interest form
2. Once 30 homes are confirmed, quotes are gathered from 2–3 local vendors
3. Group votes on the best deal via WhatsApp poll
4. Each household pays their vendor directly — no pooled money, no middleman

## About

This is a community initiative. No one is collecting money or signing contracts on anyone's behalf. All pricing is finalized once vendors are approached with a confirmed group size.
